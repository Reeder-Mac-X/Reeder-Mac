## 🖌 Overview  

Reeder Professional Suite is a thoughtfully crafted news and content reading platform that brings discipline and beauty to the act of following the web, replacing the noise of algorithmic feeds with a curated, personally controlled reading environment.

The gesture-driven navigation makes moving through feeds and articles fluid and intuitive, with swipe actions for marking, starring, and sharing that feel natural enough to become muscle memory after a short time.

Smart speed reading mode progressively reveals words at a pace you set, letting you move through long articles more quickly without losing comprehension, useful when your reading queue grows large.

iCloud sync of custom settings and local preferences ensures the app feels consistent and personal across all the devices where you read, without requiring a separate account or service.

Reeder Professional Suite is the content consumption tool for readers who want their news and feed experience to be intentional, organized, and genuinely enjoyable rather than anxious and endless.
